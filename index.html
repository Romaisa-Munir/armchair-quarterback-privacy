Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 Feb 2025

Introduction

This privacy policy explains how our running timing app uses device permissions and handles user data.

Camera Usage

Our app uses the camera permission exclusively for timing runners as they cross the finish line. The camera is used to: - Capture finish line crossings for accurate timing - Record finish times of runners - No photos or videos are stored permanently - Camera feed is processed in real-time for timing purposes only

Data Collection

We collect only: - Race timing data - Runner finish times - No personal identification information is collected through the camera

Data Storage

All timing data is stored locally on your device. We do not upload or share camera feeds or images.
